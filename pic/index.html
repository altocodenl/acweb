    Why we built ac;pic

    Why make yet another photo and video organization tool?

    Because we couldn’t find the tool we needed. A tool that allows us to see everything we have. That helps us stay organized. Simple, no complex interface, and no install. Where we can upload from our devices and cloud services. That eliminates duplicates, that respects the chronological order, that doesn’t compromise the quality of the images and videos. That requires minimal effort to use.

    That’s why we built ac;pic.

    Of course you have questions…

    Tags instead of folders? Why?

    Think of folders as boxes. Each pic must fall into an existing box or a new one must be created. A picture cannot be in multiple folders, but it can be in multiple tags. It is less of a commitment to tag a picture than to put it in a folder. Tags allow for searching by more than one tag, so searching becomes additive and requires less structure from you.

    Our brains don’t work well with boxes - we did the research. That’s why organizing with folders takes a lot of time or doesn’t even happen at all.

    Learn more
    Do I have to tag everything myself?

    Thankfully not! ac;pic has a mix of automatic and manual tags. We automatically tag your photos and videos with the year, month, geolocation (optional), containing folder and source. And then, of course, you can add all the tags you want. You can even edit or delete some of the automatic tags if you want.

    It doesn’t matter if you have 300 or 30,000 photos, ac;pic’s organization system is scalable. Your pics will always be tagged, organized and easy to find.

    What about old photos or different formats?

    ac;pic supports .jpeg, .png, .bmp, .heic, .gif, .tiff, .webp, .mp4, .mov, .3gp, .avi, .webm, .wmv and .m4v. The format you need is not supported? Let us know!

    Can I download the original files?

    Download the photos and videos you want, when you want. Want them all? No problem. And the quality of the photos and videos is never compromised. You can always access the original.

    Is this free?

    We have a free and a paid plan. The free plan gives you 5 GB with all the features, we don’t limit what you can do.

    If you need more space, with our paid plan you pay for the gigabytes you use above those free 5 GB, plus a fixed amount that enables us to maintain ac;pic.

    We run a fully transparent pricing model. Our incentives and the users' incentives are 100% aligned.
